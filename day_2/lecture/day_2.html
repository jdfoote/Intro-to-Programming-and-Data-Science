  • Has different types, e.g.:
    • Strings
    • Integers
    • Floats

Conditionals

  • Control flow
    • Run different parts of the code depending on the “state”
  • Conditionals depend on booleans
    • Expressions that evaluate to True or False
    • <, >, >=, <=, ==, !=, in, or not in
    • They are evaluated in order and can “short circuit”
